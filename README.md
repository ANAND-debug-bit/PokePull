<h3>Why I made this website ?</h3>

<hr>

<p>
  I made this website because as a pokemon fan I want to play a quick realistic offline pokemon cards game with my friends while having scores on each pokemon card based on their tiers .

<h2>Some cool features</h2>

<ul>
  <li> 5 rarity tiers (Common, Uncommon, Rare, Ultra Rare, Secret) each with their own weighted pull odds and point values</li>
  <li> Automatic score tracking and adding for both players across all rounds.</li>
  <li> Rename player names so you can personalize each side of the match</li>
  <li> A live pull log for each player showing every card, its rarity, and the points it earned</li>
  <li> Player turn highlighting indicators showing the turn of each player</li>
  <li> A dedicated end-game screen with a final scoreboard and a "play again" option</li>
  <li> Pokemon images alongside after clicking on roll</li>
</ul>
